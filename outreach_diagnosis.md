# Aiscite Outreach Diagnosis — AUT-94

## Current Template Audit

**Subject:** "Your {biz_type_label} and AI search - a private review"

**Body:**
```
Hi,

I put together a short AI visibility review for {business_name} - it takes about 2 minutes to read.

When someone asks an AI assistant for a {biz_type_label} in {city}, how your site is structured right now means AI either skips you or mentions you without confidence. There are one or two fixable gaps holding it back.

The full breakdown is here: {live_url}

No form, no pitch - just the analysis. If it is useful and you want to talk it through, there is a link to book a 10-minute call at the top.

Best
VM
```

---

## Why Zero Replies — Root Cause Analysis

### 1. Subject Line Fails the "Would I Open This?" Test
- "AI search" is abstract — partners don't wake up worried about AI
- Missing stakes: no competitor threat, no revenue implication
- Reads like a newsletter, not urgent intelligence

### 2. Value Prop Backwards
- Opens with what WE did ("I put together") instead of what THEY lose
- "AI either skips you or mentions you without confidence" — vague, no teeth
- No competitor comparison — where's the pain of losing to the firm down the street?

### 3. No Credibility Markers
- Zero social proof — no "we helped X firm get mentioned in ChatGPT"
- No numbers — how many firms audited? how many fixes implemented?
- Sender is unknown — why should they trust VM?

### 4. "No form, no pitch" is Defensive
- Sounds like we're apologizing for reaching out
- Confident outreach owns the value: "This matters. Here's why."

### 5. One Template for Two Audiences
- Law firms care about: case intake, competitor positioning, billable hours
- Med spas care about: booked procedures, ad spend waste, local visibility
- Same generic copy fails both

---

## What Would Steve Jobs Do?

1. **Start with the pain they feel** — not the solution we built
2. **Make it personal** — name their competitor, name their loss
3. **Cut everything that doesn't serve the core value** — every word must earn its place
4. **Ship with a metric** — we'll track reply rate, not just send count

---

## Recommended Action

Write two separate templates:
1. **Law Firm Template** — competitor threat, case intake, professional credibility
2. **Med Spa Template** — booked procedures, ad spend ROI, local visibility

Test both with 5-10 sends each. Measure reply rate. Iterate.
