How is this different from the free report?

The free walkthrough gives you a high-level overview of your AI visibility gaps. The Full Audit is a complete diagnostic: a scored breakdown across all major AI platforms, a competitor gap analysis against up to five competitors, a 12-month improvement roadmap, and a 30-minute call to walk you through the findings. It is built for businesses that want to act, not just get a snapshot.

How long does delivery take?

Your audit report is delivered within 48 hours of your kickoff call. Monthly monitoring reports are delivered on a rolling monthly schedule. If anything needs clarification before we begin, we will reach out quickly to keep things moving.

What if I am not satisfied?

If you complete the Full Audit and feel the report did not deliver clear, actionable value, reach out within 7 days and we will make it right. Your satisfaction matters to us and we stand behind the quality of our work. Monthly monitoring can be cancelled at any time with no penalties.
