Everything you need to know before getting started.

What is included in the audit?

The audit covers a full scan of how major AI assistants including ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and Perplexity currently describe and cite your business. You receive a scored breakdown across key visibility dimensions, a written findings report identifying your specific gaps, and a competitor comparison showing where you stand relative to similar businesses in your market.

How long does implementation take?

The audit report is delivered within 48 hours of your kickoff call. Full implementation is completed within 5 business days. The bundle includes a 30 day follow up period so we can verify that the changes are improving your AI visibility scores over time.

Do you guarantee results?

AI search is probabilistic and no one can guarantee exact ranking outcomes. What we do guarantee is that every recommendation is based on documented AI citation patterns and real visibility data from your industry. If you complete the audit and feel the findings did not deliver clear, actionable value, reach out within 7 days and we will make it right.
