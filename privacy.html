Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

When you submit the contact form or request an audit, we collect your name, email address, and business name. We do not collect payment information directly — payments are processed by Stripe.

How we use it

Your information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and deliver your audit. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties. We do not send unsolicited marketing emails.

Analytics

This site uses PostHog for product analytics. PostHog collects anonymised usage data including page views and interactions. You can opt out via PostHog's opt-out mechanism. We use this data only to improve the product — not for advertising.

Forms and submissions

Contact form submissions are processed by FormSubmit and delivered to our team email. FormSubmit stores submissions temporarily as part of their delivery process.

Cookies

We store one preference in your browser's localStorage: your light or dark mode choice. This is not a tracking cookie and does not leave your device.

Your rights

You may request deletion of any data we hold about you by emailing info@aiscite.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Contact

Questions about this policy: info@aiscite.com
