04 + 05 · Gaps and exact fixes

3 specific changes. Each one includes what to write and where to put it.

Critical · Fix this first

Specialty expertise is invisible to AI

⏱ 2 hours to fix

Carlton Dental is a specialist in airway dentistry and myofunctional therapy. AI does not know this. The words appear on the site but not in the structured, prominent way that AI needs to extract and use them. This is why Carlton disappears from its own specialty queries.

What AI currently extracts
"Carlton Dental — dental care, Toronto. General dentistry services. Patient reviews positive." — No specialty signal extracted.
Exact fix — 3 steps
  • Add a dedicated section near the top of the homepage titled "Our Specialty" or "What We Focus On" — 2 to 3 sentences in plain language naming airway dentistry and myofunctional therapy explicitly
  • Add a standalone page at /airway-dentistry/ with a clear H1, a 150-word plain-English explanation of what it is, who it helps, and why Carlton Dental focuses on it
  • Add 5 FAQ items on that page answering the exact questions patients ask before booking: "What is airway dentistry?", "How do I know if I need it?", "Is it covered by insurance?", "How long does treatment take?", "What age does this work for?"
Ready-to-use copy for homepage section:

"Carlton Dental specializes in airway dentistry and myofunctional therapy — two connected areas of dental care that affect how you breathe, sleep, and function every day. Most dental practices treat teeth. We also look at the system behind them: the jaw, the airway, and the muscles that govern both. If you or your child has been told about mouth breathing, snoring, grinding, or jaw tension, this is the right place."
Critical · Fix second

No FAQ content — AI cannot answer patient questions from your site

⏱ 3 hours to fix

When a prospective patient asks AI a question about airway dentistry or finding a Toronto dentist, AI looks for a site that already answers those questions clearly. Carlton Dental's site does not have a FAQ section. This means AI cannot use Carlton Dental as the answer source — it finds a competitor who does.

Exact fix — add these 8 questions to the site
  • "What is myofunctional therapy and does Carlton Dental offer it?" — Answer: yes, explain in 2 sentences
  • "Is Carlton Dental accepting new patients?" — Answer: yes, with booking link
  • "Does Carlton Dental treat children for airway issues?" — Answer: yes/no with age range
  • "What should I expect at my first appointment?" — Answer: 3 to 4 sentences walking through the visit
  • "How is airway dentistry different from regular dentistry?" — Answer: 2 to 3 sentences, plain English
  • "Does insurance cover airway dentistry treatment?" — Answer: honest, specific
  • "Where is Carlton Dental located and what are the hours?" — Answer: address, hours, parking note
  • "Can Carlton Dental help with teeth grinding or jaw pain?" — Answer: yes, explain connection to airway
Ready-to-use copy for one FAQ item:

"What is myofunctional therapy and does Carlton Dental offer it?

Myofunctional therapy is a series of exercises that retrain the muscles of the tongue, lips, and face. It is used to correct mouth breathing, improve swallowing patterns, and support jaw development in children. Carlton Dental offers myofunctional therapy as part of our airway-focused care program. Treatment is typically 12 to 26 sessions and can be done in person or via telehealth."
High value · Fix third

Brand summary missing — AI builds a generic description of the practice

⏱ 30 minutes to fix

AI needs a clear, concise summary of what Carlton Dental is and what makes it different. Without one, it pieces together a generic version from scattered text. The fix is a single paragraph — placed in the footer or About section — that tells AI exactly how to describe this practice.

Exact fix — add this text to the site footer and About page
"Carlton Dental is a Toronto dental practice specializing in airway dentistry and myofunctional therapy. Founded by [Dr. Name], the practice focuses on the relationship between oral health, breathing, and whole-body function. Carlton Dental serves patients of all ages with a particular focus on children and adults with airway-related concerns including mouth breathing, snoring, sleep-disordered breathing, and jaw development issues. Located in [neighbourhood], Toronto."
  • Place this verbatim in the website footer (small text is fine — AI reads it)
  • Use it as the opening paragraph of the About page
  • Add it as the meta description on the homepage
