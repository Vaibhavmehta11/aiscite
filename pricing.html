FAQ

Questions about pricing and what is included.

Can I start with just the audit?

Yes. The GEO Audit is fully standalone. You get the full score, issue breakdown, and priority fix list. Many clients run the audit first to confirm the opportunity is real before deciding on implementation.

Is the monthly service a contract?

No. AI Signal Management is month-to-month with no minimum term. Cancel with 30 days notice. You are not locked in.

What if I have multiple locations?

Each location has its own AI visibility profile — a shared website does not mean shared AI signals. Each location is audited and billed separately. Contact us if you have more than two locations and we will scope it together.

What is included in the free walkthrough?

A 10-minute call where we confirm your business type, scope, and whether the audit makes sense for your situation. No pitch. If it is not a good fit, we will tell you.

How is this different from SEO?

Traditional SEO focuses on search rankings. Aiscite focuses on how AI assistants understand, describe, and recommend your business when someone asks them a question. It is a different problem that needs a different approach.

Do I need a developer to implement the fixes?

For the audit, no — you get prioritized fixes in plain English. For the GEO Build, we handle all implementation. If you are doing it yourself, most fixes are copy and content changes, not code.
